## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open, welcoming, and growth-oriented environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in the HabitFlow project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

Just as we believe in the power of small, consistent habits to create lasting change, we believe in the power of small, consistent acts of kindness and respect to build a thriving community.

## ‚úÖ Expected Behavior

We encourage all community members to:

### In Communication
- **Be Kind**: Use welcoming, respectful, and encouraging language
- **Be Patient**: Help others learn and grow, regardless of their experience level
- **Be Constructive**: Provide helpful feedback and suggestions
- **Be Professional**: Maintain a respectful tone in all interactions
- **Be Inclusive**: Use language that welcomes everyone

### In Collaboration
- **Be Collaborative**: Work together to solve problems and build features
- **Be Transparent**: Communicate openly about your work and challenges
- **Be Reliable**: Follow through on commitments and communicate delays
- **Be Receptive**: Accept feedback gracefully and consider different viewpoints
- **Be Supportive**: Help newcomers get started and feel welcome

### In Technical Discussions
- **Focus on Ideas**: Critique ideas, not people
- **Provide Context**: Explain your reasoning and share relevant information
- **Ask Questions**: Seek to understand before seeking to be understood
- **Share Knowledge**: Help others learn through clear explanations
- **Acknowledge Others**: Give credit where credit is due

## ‚ùå Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered harassment and are unacceptable:

### Personal Attacks
- Insulting, demeaning, or attacking comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Trolling or inflammatory comments

### Discrimination
- Discriminatory language or actions
- Exclusionary jokes or comments
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Intimidating or threatening behavior

### Unprofessional Conduct
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Deliberately spreading misinformation
- Spamming or excessive self-promotion
- Disruptive behavior in discussions or events

### Technical Misconduct
- Submitting malicious code or content
- Deliberately breaking the project or infrastructure
- Violating security policies or user privacy
- Copyright infringement or plagiarism

## üõ†Ô∏è Enforcement

### Reporting Guidelines

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by contacting us at:

- **Email**: salman@scrafi.dev
- **GitHub**: [Create a private issue](https://github.com/MSCRAFI/habitflow/issues/new)
- **Direct Contact**: Reach out to any project maintainer

When reporting, please include:
- **What happened**: Clear description of the incident
- **When and where**: Date, time, and context of the behavior
- **Who was involved**: People involved (if comfortable sharing)
- **Impact**: How the behavior affected you or others

### Investigation Process

1. **Acknowledgment**: We will acknowledge receipt within 24 hours
2. **Investigation**: We will review the report thoroughly and fairly
3. **Response**: We will respond with our findings and any actions taken
4. **Follow-up**: We will check in to ensure the issue is resolved

All reports will be handled with discretion and confidentiality.

### Enforcement Actions

Project maintainers have the right and responsibility to take appropriate action in response to violations. Actions may include:

#### Warning
- **First offense** or **minor violation**
- Private discussion about the behavior
- Guidance on expected community standards

#### Temporary Suspension
- **Repeated violations** or **moderate offense**
- Temporary removal from community spaces
- Limited interaction privileges for specified period

#### Permanent Ban
- **Severe violations** or **repeated serious offenses**
- Permanent removal from all community spaces
- Prohibition from future participation

### Appeal Process

If you believe an enforcement action was taken in error:
1. Contact salman@scrafi.dev within 30 days
2. Provide a clear explanation of your perspective
3. Include any relevant evidence or context
4. Allow 14 days for review and response
